## Feasibility Analysis

### High Feasibility (>80%)
Services with structured inputs, clear decision criteria, and low risk:
- **SVC-006**: Proposal summarization (text processing)
- **SVC-007**: Voting monitoring (state observation)
- **SVC-011**: Anomaly detection (statistical analysis)
- **SVC-012**: Liquidity monitoring (metrics computation)
- **SVC-016**: Validator tracking (performance metrics)

### Medium Feasibility (60-80%)
Services requiring domain knowledge or multi-step reasoning:
- **SVC-001**: Credit class screening (methodology analysis)
- **SVC-003**: Batch review (verification logic)
- **SVC-015**: Quality scoring (subjective elements)
- **SVC-018**: Decentralization assessment (complex metrics)

### Lower Feasibility (<60%)
Services requiring deep expertise or high-stakes decisions:
- **SVC-004**: Methodology compliance (expert judgment)
- **SVC-009**: Parameter optimization (economic modeling)

## Recommendations

1. **Phase 3 Priority**: Implement SVC-006, SVC-007, SVC-011, SVC-016 first (highest feasibility)
2. **Human Oversight**: Maintain human-in-loop for SVC-003, SVC-004 (high stakes)
3. **Gradual Rollout**: Start with monitoring/alerting, progress to recommendation, then automation
4. **Feedback Loops**: Implement confidence scoring and accuracy tracking for continuous improvement
